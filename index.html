12 Year ASE Master Technician. Jay spent over ten years turning a wrench professionally, Working for manufacturers such as Subaru, Mitsubishi, Hyundai, Kia, Chevrolet and GMC. Now, he focuses on custom Motorcycle work and Hot rodding in his spare time.

Always having an interest in art, Jay has pursued comic book style illustration and tattoo design recreationally for over 17 years. He has previously designed Album artwork for local Punk bands, Illustrated small projects at Fresno City College, and has helped design over 20 Tattoos (not including his own.)

Starting in 2021, Jay decided to pursue an education in something other than Automotive work, one that aligned more with his core values of focusing on his growing family. Currently enrolled in Web Development courses, Jay will continue with JavaScript in January at BitWise Industries.
